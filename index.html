About Us

Eden Labrys Audio is a trans-owned and operated recording production company based in Bloomington, Indiana. Our purpose is to improve the economic stability of LGBTQ artists by connecting clients with queer audio engineers, session musicians, and artists.

Recording, mixing, and mastering services are available at Echo Park Studios, located northwest of downtown. Live recording services are available regionally.
